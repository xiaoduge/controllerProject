RephiLink Service Privacy Policy

This page is used to inform you regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Information if you decided to use this service.

To grant you access and to use this service, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The Information that we collect is used for providing and improving our service. We will not use or share your information with anyone or a third party unless you authorize in our RephiLink APP. RephiLink APP's terms and conditions and Privacy Policy are defined when you install the RephiLink APP.

If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, we will not register your system, and nor allow the system to send system data to our server to use this service.

Information Collection and Use

For a better service, identification of you system, and water system operation data will be sent from your system to our Server periodically, or when there is an active alarm or alert. The information we receive will be retained by us and used as described in this Privacy Policy. System information is uploaded when you check to activate the function, and the system is linked to the internet via Wi-Fi or Ethernet.

Log Data

We want to inform you that once the function is activated, your water system will upload system status data and identification periodically or in a case of alarms and alerts. This Log Data may include information such as your device name, operating system version, the operation status, the time and date of your use of the water system, and other statistics.

Service Providers

We use a third-party hosting service such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) to store system history data due to the following reasons:

  • To store data collected;
  • To relay data to your authorized email addresses via RephiLink APP;
  • To perform related services by our service engineer, such as diagnose system status and issues when we receive service calls from you.
